Welcome to the Hospital Management System

A Comprehensive Solution for Hospital Operations

The Hospital Management System is designed to streamline and improve efficiency in hospital operations, from patient registrations to managing appointments, doctors, rooms, prescriptions, and invoices.

Key features include:

  • - Register and manage patient and doctor details
  • - Schedule and track appointments
  • - Assign rooms to patients and monitor availability
  • - Manage prescriptions and generate invoices for patients

Use the navigation bar above to access different functionalities of the system. It's easy to use, quick to navigate, and efficient in improving hospital workflows.
